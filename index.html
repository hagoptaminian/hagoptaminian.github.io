A story

“Ludwig,” Hermine said, “I cannot understand it. The Lord gave you such a fine mind, such stunning intelligence. And after Cambridge and the professorship... I simply cannot understand it, why you’re wasting it all like this, here, teaching at an elementary school.”

“Hermine, you remind me of someone who is looking through a shaded window and cannot explain to herself the strange movements of a passerby. She does not realize that there is a storm raging outside and that the passerby is only with great difficulty managing to keep himself on his feet.”
